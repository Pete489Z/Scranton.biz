Total Long term Debt



Debt Ratio

Simply total liabilities divided by total assets. The lower the ratio, the less debt it has compared to its assets. Lower better.



Times-Interest Earned

Revenue/Interest paid per year. Ratio expressing number of times the yearly interest payment can be paid. Excludes $66.5 million from sewer sale. Higher better.



Revenue to Debt Ratio


Revenue/Total Long Term Debt. Ratio showing the percent of debt that can be payed off using the entirety of a given year's revenue. Excludes $66.5 million from sewer sale. Higher better.
